TaftTasks

Get set up in under 5 minutes

1

Create your account

Go to tafttasks.pages.dev and sign up with your Taft email address. You'll get a confirmation email — click the link inside to activate your account.

Note: Use your @taftschool.org email so your account is tied to Taft.
2

Install the Chrome extension

Open the Chrome Web Store and search for TaftTasks. Click Add to Chrome, then Add extension to confirm. The TaftTasks icon will appear in your toolbar.

Tip: Pin the extension so it's always visible — click the puzzle piece icon in the toolbar, then the pin next to TaftTasks.
3

Sign in to the extension

Click the TaftTasks icon in your toolbar. Enter the email and password you used in Step 1. You only need to do this once — the extension stays signed in.

4

Go to a Canvas Modules page

Open Canvas, navigate to any course, and click Modules in the left sidebar.

Repeat for each course: You'll import one course at a time — takes about 10 seconds per course.
5

Import your assignments

Click the TaftTasks icon. The extension will detect your assignments and show a summary. Click Import to Dashboard. Done — your assignments are now in TaftTasks.

✓ You're done

Open your dashboard

All your assignments, due dates, and progress in one place. Check off items as you finish them.

Open TaftTasks →

Do I need to re-import after Canvas updates?

Yes — visit the Modules page and click Import again to pick up new assignments. Existing items won't be duplicated.

Will this work on any Canvas course?

Yes, as long as the course uses the Modules view. If a teacher uses a different layout, some assignments may not be detected.

Is my data private?

Your assignments are stored in your personal account only. No one else can see your data.
